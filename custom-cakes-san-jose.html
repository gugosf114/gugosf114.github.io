San Jose's Premier Custom Cake Bakery

Looking for a custom cake in San Jose? My Baking Creations has been Silicon Valley's trusted bakery for stunning birthday cakes, elegant wedding cakes, and creative corporate treats since 2012. We proudly deliver throughout San Jose—from the upscale shops of Santana Row and Valley Fair to the charming tree-lined streets of Willow Glen, the historic Japantown, and the family neighborhoods of Almaden Valley and Evergreen.

San Jose celebrations deserve extraordinary cakes. Whether you're hosting a quinceañera in East San Jose, a wedding reception at the Fairmont or Hotel Valencia, or a milestone birthday near the SAP Center, our custom cakes become the centerpiece of your event. We've created memorable treats for celebrations at the San Jose Museum of Art, garden parties in Rose Garden neighborhood, and tech company events throughout Downtown. Our sculpted 3D cakes and ultra-realistic designs bring wonder to every San Jose celebration.

As the Capital of Silicon Valley, San Jose is home to major tech headquarters that trust us for their corporate celebrations. From Adobe and Cisco to PayPal and eBay, we deliver custom logo cookies, branded cake pops, and celebration cakes for product launches, quarterly celebrations, and employee appreciation events. Whether your office is in North San Jose's tech corridor or the emerging Downtown district, we bring treats that reflect your company's innovation and success.
