Corporate Event Dessert Ideas

Impress clients and employees with memorable branded treats

Corporate events are opportunities to make lasting impressions on clients, celebrate employee achievements, and reinforce your brand identity. The right desserts can transform an ordinary meeting into a memorable experience. Here's how Silicon Valley's leading companies are elevating their events with custom branded desserts.

Why Branded Desserts Work for Corporate Events

When Google, Meta, Salesforce, and other Fortune 500 companies order custom desserts for their events, they're investing in more than just sweets. Branded desserts serve multiple purposes:

  • Brand reinforcement: Every bite reminds guests of your company
  • Social media moments: Beautifully branded treats get photographed and shared
  • Thoughtful touch: Custom desserts show attention to detail and care
  • Conversation starters: Unique treats spark networking conversations
  • Memorable takeaways: Individually wrapped items become gifts guests remember

Top Corporate Dessert Options

Logo Cookies

The most versatile and popular choice for corporate events. Your company logo is printed in full color on delicious sugar cookies using food-safe edible ink.

Best for: Client gifts, trade shows, product launches, holiday parties, employee welcome packages

Pricing: $5-$8 per cookie, depending on size and complexity

Packaging options: Individual cellophane bags with ribbon, gift boxes, or displayed on platters

Branded Cake Pops

Bite-sized cake on a stick, dipped in chocolate and decorated with your brand colors. Cake pops are easy to eat while networking and photograph beautifully.

Best for: Conferences, networking events, product launches, office celebrations

Pricing: $5-$10 per cake pop, depending on decoration complexity

Customization: Match any brand color, add edible logo stickers, custom drizzles

Custom Cupcakes

Elegantly frosted cupcakes topped with edible logo toppers or hand-piped decorations in your brand colors. No cutting required—guests can grab and go.

Best for: Office parties, team celebrations, client meetings, milestone events

Pricing: $5-$8 per cupcake

Display: Tiered stands create impressive dessert stations

Celebration Cakes

Larger centerpiece cakes decorated with your company logo, product images, or celebration messages. Perfect for milestone celebrations that call for a ceremonial cake cutting.

Best for: Company anniversaries, major deal closings, retirement parties, product launches

Pricing: $250-$650+ depending on size and design

Stand out on the exhibition floor:

Product Launches

Planning Your Corporate Dessert Order

How Many to Order

For events with other food, plan for 1-2 pieces per person. For dessert-focused events, plan for 2-3 pieces per person. Always order 10-15% extra for unexpected guests or seconds.

Lead Time

  • Standard orders (under 100 pieces): 2 weeks
  • Large orders (100-500 pieces): 3 weeks
  • Very large orders (500+ pieces): 4+ weeks
  • Rush orders: Sometimes available with additional fee

What We Need From You

  • High-resolution logo file (PNG, AI, or EPS preferred)
  • Brand color codes (Pantone or hex codes)
  • Event date and delivery/pickup details
  • Quantity needed

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best desserts for corporate events?

The best corporate event desserts are individually portioned, easy to serve, and can be branded with your company logo. Popular options include logo cookies, branded cake pops, cupcakes, and mini dessert bars. These are easy for guests to grab and go while networking.

How do you put a company logo on cookies?

Company logos are printed on cookies using edible ink on edible paper, which is then applied to royal icing-covered sugar cookies. The result is a crisp, full-color reproduction of your logo that's completely food-safe and delicious.

How far in advance should I order corporate desserts?

For corporate orders, we recommend 2-3 weeks advance notice for standard orders and 3-4 weeks for large orders (100+ pieces) or complex custom designs. Rush orders may be available with an additional fee.

Can corporate desserts be individually wrapped?

Yes! Individual wrapping is available for cookies and cake pops, making them perfect for gift bags, trade show giveaways, or shipping to remote employees. Individual wrapping helps with freshness and makes distribution easy.

Do you deliver to Silicon Valley offices?

Yes! We deliver throughout the Bay Area including San Francisco, San Jose, Palo Alto, Mountain View, Cupertino, Menlo Park, Redwood City, and all surrounding areas. We've delivered to Google, Meta, Salesforce, and many other tech company campuses.
