San Francisco's Premier Custom Cake Bakery

Looking for a custom cake in San Francisco? My Baking Creations has been the Bay Area's trusted bakery for stunning birthday cakes, elegant wedding cakes, and creative corporate treats since 2012. Based just minutes from the city in Daly City, we deliver throughout San Francisco—from the iconic neighborhoods of the Mission and Castro to the bustling Financial District and waterfront Embarcadero.

San Francisco celebrations deserve extraordinary cakes. Whether you're hosting a birthday party in Pacific Heights, a wedding reception at City Hall or the Ferry Building, or a corporate event in SoMa, our custom cakes become the centerpiece of your celebration. We've created stunning treats for events near the Golden Gate Bridge, Salesforce Tower launch parties, and intimate gatherings in Noe Valley homes. San Francisco's tech companies trust us for their employee appreciation events, client gifts, and milestone celebrations. From startups in the Dogpatch to Fortune 500 headquarters downtown, we deliver custom logo cookies, branded cake pops, and celebration cakes that reflect your company culture.
