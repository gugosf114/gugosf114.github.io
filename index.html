Why Choose My Baking Creations

Looking for the best custom cake bakery in the San Francisco Bay Area? My Baking Creations has been the trusted choice for custom birthday cakes, wedding cakes, corporate logo cookies, and branded cake pops since 2012. Known locally for our ultra-realistic, highly artistic cake designs—every creation is edible art. Our family-owned bakery in Daly City serves San Francisco, Silicon Valley, San Jose, Marin County, and the entire Bay Area with delivery available.

We specialize in sculpted 3D cakes that become the centerpiece of your celebration, ultra-realistic cakes that fool your guests, elegant tiered wedding cakes, and hand-decorated sugar cookies with royal icing. Our corporate clients—including Google, Meta, Salesforce, Microsoft, and PayPal—trust us for employee appreciation gifts, client thank-you packages, product launch celebrations, and company milestone events.

Every custom cake, cookie, and cake pop is handcrafted with premium ingredients and personalized attention. Whether you're planning a milestone birthday party, wedding reception, baby shower, corporate event, or holiday celebration, we bring your vision to life with edible artistry.

Order your custom cake, cookies, or cake pops today! Contact us for a free consultation and quote. We typically book 2-3 weeks in advance for custom orders.
